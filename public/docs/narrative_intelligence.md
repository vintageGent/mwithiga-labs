# Narrative Intelligence: Strategic Signal Analysis

## 📋 Capability Overview
The **Narrative Intelligence** module (powered by the PR Starter Kit and LeDe engines) is designed for organizations that need to communicate complex technical risk or strategic milestones with authority and precision.

## 🛠️ Core Capabilities

### 1. Strategic Signal Crafting
- Drafting high-authority press releases and executive dispatches.
- Ensuring consistency across technical documentation and public-facing narratives.

### 2. Reputation Risk Mitigation
- Crafting incident response communications that maintain institutional trust.
- Translating complex hardening efforts into "Strategic Signals" for stakeholders.

### 3. PR Performance Audit
- Analyzing the narrative impact of technical milestones within the industry landscape.

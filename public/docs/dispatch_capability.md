## Strategic Intelligence Core

Dispatch is a high-performance intelligence engine that audits digital surfaces to identify verified institutional connection points including email addresses, phone numbers, and social media profiles.

Under the Data Protection Act (2019), Privacy by Design requirements mandate that organizations minimize accidental exposure of sensitive contact data. Dispatch enables institutions to audit their own digital surface exposure and standardize outreach through verified, secure channels, thereby reducing the risk of phishing and impersonation.

By transforming raw contact harvesting into formal Strategic Communications Dossiers, Dispatch provides the technical evidence required to justify institutional contact pivots and standardized communication protocols.

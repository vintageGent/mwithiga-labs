# Sentinel Core: The Strategic Intelligence Operating System

## 📋 Strategic Overview
**The Sentinel Core** is the proprietary intelligence engine that orchestrates the entire Mwithiga Labs toolset. It is designed to host, execute, and analyze technical risk findings, translating raw operational logs into executive-level strategic dispatches.

## 🛠️ Core Architecture

### 1. Intelligence Orchestration
- Unified control plane for **ThreatScope**, **Portal**, and **Dispatch**.
- Automated work-stream sequencing for complex audits and hardening projects.

### 2. Risk Reasoning Engine
- Heuristic analysis of technical findings against business risk models.
- Automated generation of "Executive Notes" and "Remediation Roadmaps".

### 3. Compliance Mapping
- Real-time correlation of technical evidence against the **Data Protection Act (2019)** framework.
- Evidence-ready output generation for DPO-level reporting.

## 💼 Implementation
The Sentinel Core exists as a proprietary desktop environment (available for authorized advisory clients) that ensures all technical work is mapped to high-authority strategic outcomes.
